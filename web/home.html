Perry Georgia

I was raised in Perry, Georgia. It was always my own little slice of perry-dise. Perry has a population of 14,000 people, the perfect size. It's small enough to have the small town feel with a quaint historic area full of mom and pop shops, but big enough that not everyone is in your business. We are the proud location of The Georgia National Fair, so for one week every year, the big city comes to us. It was an amazing place to grow up.
